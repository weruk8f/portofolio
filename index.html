{ me&other celestial bodies //publication }
Personal exploration of bridges between arts and science.
It looks for traces of human need of understanding. Not proven
or proven wrong science theories are being translated into
speculative narrations and poems of worlds, in which those theories
were correct. It's a story of how we look for the answer. Project
is connecting arts and science in their common purpose of explaining
the world around and between us.

{ WHEN I GROW UP, I WANT TO BE A MINER //interactive video / video-game(?) }
Based in the early 80s of the 20th century, the project tells
history based narration on Polish coal mining industry. On
this example, it explains how propaganda tools were used
(in this case) to create an certain image of coal, mining,
and miners, and shows how it raised a whole generation into
still lively beliefs. Comparing that later on with nowadays
media communication it asks questions of use of influnece
methods.
